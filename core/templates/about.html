About Leela Foundation

"एक कदम आप बढ़ाइए, हजारों कदम समाज आपके साथ बढ़ेगा।" We believe in creating a ripple effect of change that lasts for generations.

"इंसानियत से बड़ा कोई धर्म नहीं होता।"

Leela Foundation was founded with a single goal: to empower communities through education, health, and sustainable development. We believe in the power of localized action to create global impact.

The Vision

Creating a world where every community has the resources to thrive independently.

The Mission

Implementing sustainable solutions for education and health at the grassroots level.
